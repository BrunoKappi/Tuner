How does the high-precision detection work?

To eliminate typical web tuner fluctuations caused by string transient harmonics and background noise, Bkappi Tuner implements three layers of digital signal processing:

  • 1
    RMS Amplitude Gate:

    Calculates the Root Mean Square of the captured buffer to block ambient background noise from triggering false notes.

  • 2
    Exponential Moving Average (EMA):

    Applies a mathematical low-pass filter (α = 0.18) to the frequency needle. It detects frequency jumps > 8% and resets instantly to ensure instantaneous responsiveness on real changes.

  • 3
    Statistical Mode Filtering:

    Maintains a circular buffer of the last 12 readings and outputs the statistical Mode. This completely dampens harmonic peaks and delivers solid, stable pitch feedback.
